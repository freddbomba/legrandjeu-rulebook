Sheet 1 / 4 · LGJ Collective Events · Fronts
Print on A3 · cut on dashed lines
LGJ 01Policy
Universal Basic Income
A new policy guarantees an income for all. Each player receives 2 whites from the grand pot. Assemblies for the next 2 rounds may propose how it is funded.
Lower-income actors support the measure; others resent it.
LGJ 02Economic
Hyperinflation
All operation costs rise by 50% for the next 3 rounds. Round-to-round yields are unchanged.
Rising tensions as people struggle to afford necessities; potential for unrest.
LGJ 03Environment
Bioregion Chemical Takeover
A neighbouring bioregion shifts to chemical-intensive cereal production. Atmosphere pot gains 3 greys. Any farm in this bioregion loses 1 white of yield next round.
Community splits over environmental concerns; protests may arise.
LGJ 04Environment
Positive Carbon Trading
A new carbon trading initiative boosts the local economy. Remove 3 greys from the atmosphere pot. Each player with a clean activity gains 1 white.
Generally positive reception; community collaboration in green projects.
LGJ 05Climate
Global Warming Catastrophe
Roll 1d6 to determine the event: 1 famine · 2 flood · 3 fire · 4 drought · 5 hurricane · 6 heatwave. The GM narrates local impact. Atmosphere pot gains 5 greys.
Anxiety rises; potential for unexpected solidarity.
LGJ 06Extraction
Mining Expansion
Resource extraction increases. One off-grid territory (GM chooses) is taken over — its owner receives 3 whites compensation but loses the land. Atmosphere gains 4 greys.
Resentment from locals; potential protests against environmental degradation.
LGJ 07Demographic
Climate Migration
Newcomers arrive. The local pot gains 4 whites (labour & taxes) but housing becomes scarce. Next assembly must decide on integration policy.
Cultural blending, but also potential for xenophobia and social tension.
LGJ 08Food system
Famine from Labour Shortages
Lack of agricultural workers leads to food shortages. Each farm or food element yields half this round. Players with no food element lose 1 white.
Desperation rises; potential for community-driven food initiatives.
LGJ 09Infrastructure
Cheap Flight Airport
A new airport brings tourism. On-grid players gain 2 whites per round for 3 rounds. Atmosphere pot gains 2 greys per round for the same span.
Mixed feelings: income rises, but locals feel overwhelmed.
LGJ 10Policy
UN Regeneration Program
A global initiative based on carbon credits funds community regeneration. Local pot gains 6 whites, earmarked for projects that reduce grey.
Hope for revitalisation; potential conflicts over allocation.
LGJ 11Ideology
Death Cult
A charismatic movement preaching collapse as destiny gains followers. Social value drops by 3. Any player may receive 4 whites from the grand pot if they publicly endorse it — and gain 2 permanent greys.
Polarisation deepens; friendships split along the line.
LGJ 12Extinction
Meteor Hits Earth
Game over immediately. No one wins. The GM narrates the final minutes: who is where, who reaches whom, what is said. Then there is silence at the table.
A card designed to be drawn once in a lifetime of play.

Sheet 3 / 4 · Fortune · Personal Events · Fronts
Print on A3 · cut on dashed lines
EV 01Admin
Tax Inspector
An audit is opened on your activity. Pay 2 whites to the local pot. If you cannot, skip your next action.
Stress and potential conflict with authorities.
EV 02Wellbeing
Depression
You experience a difficult spell. Your next Develop/Survive yield is halved. You may call a single ally to act on your behalf for 1 white.
Possible isolation; friends may rally in support.
EV 03Fortune
Basic Income Doubled
Your share of basic income is doubled this round. Receive 3 whites from the grand pot.
Opportunity for community investment.
EV 04Market
Mega Shopping Centre
A new shopping centre opens near you. If you own a shop or craft element, its yield drops by 1 per round for 3 rounds.
Traditional markets strain; community divides.
EV 05Climate
Cold Winter Harvest
Harsh winter halves the yield of your farm, garden, or energy element this round.
Solidarity rises; reliance on mutual aid grows.
EV 06Mishap
Goats Destroy Everything
Goats (yours or a neighbour's) wreck a crop. Lose 1d4 whites of yield. If you blame a neighbour, they may call an assembly.
Possible neighbour disputes; negotiation required.
EV 07Climate
Flood Destroys Garden
A sudden flood devastates one of your territories. Remove any element built there. Local pot gains 2 greys.
Community may rally to help you recover.
EV 08Growth
Learn to Fly
You acquire a new skill or hobby. Gain 1 Learn action free of cost during your next turn. Describe what it is.
You may inspire others; workshops become possible.
EV 09Subsidy
Wind Power Subsidy
You receive a grant to invest in wind energy. Gain 4 whites earmarked for any element that produces no grey. Unused by end of next round, returned to local pot.
Community conversation about renewables opens up.
EV 10Crisis
Hacked
Your personal or business accounts are compromised. Lose 1d6 whites. Gain +2 on your next Learn roll (you've learned the hard way).
Trust frays within the community; cybersecurity rises on the agenda.
EV 11Ethics
Decide to Break Bad
You're tempted by questionable activity. You may gain 3 whites immediately — but the GM gives you 2 hidden greys that trigger on the next fortune card you draw.
Moral dilemmas split your circle; some will support, others will condemn.
EV 12Grace
Amy Winehouse Lives Again
Against all evidence, she is back — and playing a surprise set nearby. You are inspired. Gain +2 on your next Invent roll and 2 whites. If you choose Enjoy this round, remove an extra 1d6 greys.
A brief reprieve from the cynicism of the age. The night is long, the songs are longer.
