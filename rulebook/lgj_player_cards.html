The Round

Pay tax — if you're on the grid, put 1 white in the local pot.
Take one action — choose from the five below.
Resolve the wheel — one player spins; the GM narrates the event.

The Five Actions

Develop
1d10
Describe what you want to build on a territory. GM sets cost & yield. Roll for starting strength.
Invent
1d20
Pay research cost, roll 1d20. On success, gain a new element.
Assembly
vote
Call a collective decision. Debate, then vote by majority or consensus.
Learn
pay
Pay a cost set by the GM to gain a skill or knowledge.
Enjoy
1d6
Spend whites, burn grey, strengthen social bonds.

The Currencies

White — money, resources, knowledge, energy.
Grey — pollution, waste, depletion, entropy.
Score = Whites − Greys

Game ends when…

· The agreed rounds are played (5–8).
· Total grey on the table exceeds 50.
· Time runs out (typically 1.5–2 hrs).

If You Run Out of Whites

You're eliminated. +5 grey per remaining round goes to the atmosphere pot. Ask for help before it happens — call an assembly, trade, go off-grid.

Victory Ask the GM what counts as winning for this scenario — the rule stays hidden until someone asks.
